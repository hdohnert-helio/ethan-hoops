🍪 The BEEF Formula
Balance · Eyes · Elbow · Follow Through ▼
B
Balance
Feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent before every shot.
E
Eyes
Lock on a spot on the rim before you shoot. Don't watch the ball.
E
Elbow
Shooting elbow tucked under the ball, pointing at the basket.
F
Follow Through
Reach into the cookie jar — hold your hand up until it goes in.
📋 Parent Reminders
👈
Left hand matters

He'll resist it. Do it anyway. This is the biggest separator at youth level.

🔢
Count made shots

Kids respond to personal records. Beat yesterday's number — that's all that matters.

🏆
End with free play

Always finish by letting him shoot around freely. Keeps him wanting to come back.

😤
Don't over-coach

Just rebound for him. Reps matter more than perfect instruction at this age.

🎉
Celebrate small wins

First left-hand make, best free throw session — those moments build the love of the game.
